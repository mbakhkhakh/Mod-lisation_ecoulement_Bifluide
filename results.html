This section presents the results after solving the Transport equation and the validation of the different steps in Prediction-Correction algorithm

Transport Equation

Poisson Equation:

In order to validate the Poisson equation with a variable coefficient $ \nabla (\alpha (x,y)\nabla u)= 0 $, we retained as an exact solution $ u(x, y)= x^{2}+ 2x $. By developing and replacing $ u $ we find: $ \alpha (x) = \dfrac{1}{x+1} $. We can find a relative $ L1 $ error of $ 10^{-12} $ between our numerical solution and the predefined exact solution. This means that our model clearly converged.
